Privacy Policy

How Coinverge protects your information as an SEC-registered investment adviser.

Coinverge LLC ("Coinverge", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, engage with our services, or become a client of our SEC-registered investment advisory services focused on cryptocurrency and digital assets.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, financial information (such as investment objectives, risk tolerance, account balances, and tax identification numbers), and any other information you voluntarily provide during consultations, form submissions, or client onboarding.
  • Non-Personal / Automatically Collected Information: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and usage data collected through cookies and similar technologies for website analytics and improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized crypto investment advisory, portfolio management, and related services.
  • Communicate with you regarding consultations, reports, and market insights.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations as an SEC-registered investment adviser (including record-keeping and anti-money laundering requirements).
  • Improve our website and services through analytics.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., custodians like Coinbase Custody or Fidelity Digital Assets, tax software providers) who assist in delivering our services and are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Regulatory authorities as required by law (SEC, IRS, etc.).
  • Affiliates or in connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition).

As a Registered Investment Adviser, we follow the privacy requirements under Regulation S-P and other applicable U.S. federal and state laws.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security, especially with respect to cryptocurrency-related data and on-chain activities.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA (if applicable). We do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses essential cookies for functionality and analytics cookies (which you can manage via your browser settings). We do not use tracking for advertising purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post the revised version with the new "Last Updated" date.
