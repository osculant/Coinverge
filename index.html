Frequently Asked Questions

Is Coinverge a registered investment advisor?
Yes — Coinverge is an SEC-registered investment adviser specializing in digital assets.
What types of clients do you serve?
Accredited investors and high-net-worth individuals seeking professional crypto guidance.
How do you ensure the security of my assets?
Coinverge never takes direct custody of your private keys. We work with institutional-grade custodians like Coinbase Custody and Fidelity Digital Assets to ensure your funds are protected by multi-signature security and insurance.
Do you provide tax reporting for crypto transactions?
Yes, we provide comprehensive end-of-year tax reports (8949 forms) and integrate directly with popular tax software like CoinTracker and Koinly to make your filing process seamless.
Is there a minimum investment requirement?
To provide the most value through our active management and advisory, we typically recommend a minimum starting portfolio of $50,000 for the Starter plan.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at the end of any billing cycle. We don't believe in long-term lock-ins; our goal is to earn your trust every single month.
